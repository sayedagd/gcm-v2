Driver Lifecycle

Driver portal, documents, and missions

The driver module is mobile-first and covers profile documents, permits, mission registration, and the driver-facing trip lifecycle.

Home Docs archive System knowledge Trips Reports Deployment
  • Driver profile documents include iqama, license, operating card, and insurance.
  • Permit zones are stored as JSON data and handled defensively in the dashboard.
  • Mission registration is done through a trip wizard with proof-photo capture and assignment steps.
  • The driver journey is role-based and mobile-first.
