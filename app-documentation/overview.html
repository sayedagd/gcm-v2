Overview

What this platform does

GCM v2 is an environmental operations platform for companies that collect projects, recyclables, and waste. The core system manages companies, projects, services, trips, drivers, vehicles, suppliers, facilities, inventory, reports, AI sessions, and public landing/store flows.

Scope

System shape

The implementation is role-based and bilingual, with Arabic and English content across key modules. Public journeys cover landing, store, and contact surfaces, while internal journeys cover admin, finance, logistics, operations, and external portal users.
