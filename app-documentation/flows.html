User Flows

Role-based journeys

The product is organized around clear persona paths so each role sees the screens and actions that match its responsibilities.

Home Overview Frontend Backend Runbooks Print export
  1. Admin: configures SaaS branding, users, reports, and system controls.
  2. Operations staff: creates trips, tracks approvals, manages logistics, and monitors activity logs.
  3. Client: views project-scoped dashboards and trip summaries through the client portal.
  4. Subcontractor: reviews assigned assets and operational responsibility through the subcontractor portal.
  5. Driver: sees assigned work, document status, and mission progress in the driver portal.
